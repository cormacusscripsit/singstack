# Privacy Policy — SingStack

*Last updated: 19 April 2026*

SingStack is developed by Marschner Labs. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

---

## What data the app collects

### Google account information
When you sign in with Google, SingStack receives your Google account name and email address. This is used solely to authenticate you with Google Drive so the app can read and write files in shared project folders. Your name and email are not stored on any Marschner Labs server.

### Audio recordings
When you record a vocal part, the audio is saved locally on your device and uploaded to the Google Drive folder shared by your director. Recordings are stored only in your Google Drive account and are subject to Google's own privacy policy. Marschner Labs does not receive, store, or process your audio on its own servers.

### Nickname and voice part
You enter a nickname and voice part (e.g. Soprano, Tenor) during first-time setup. This information is stored locally on your device and embedded in the metadata file that accompanies each recording you upload to Google Drive. It is visible to other members of any Drive project you participate in.

### Crash reports
If the app crashes, an anonymised crash report is sent to Google Firebase Crashlytics. These reports include:

- The type and message of the error
- A stack trace identifying the location of the crash in the app's code
- Basic device information: Android version, device model, and available memory at the time of the crash
- A randomly generated installation identifier (not linked to your Google account or name)

Crash reports do not include your name, email address, audio recordings, or the contents of your Google Drive. They are used only to diagnose and fix bugs. Firebase Crashlytics is operated by Google; see [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

---

## What the app does not collect

- The app does not collect advertising identifiers
- The app does not share data with third parties beyond Google (Drive and Firebase)
- The app does not track your location
- The app does not access your contacts, photos, or any files outside the specific Drive folders you open

---

## Permissions

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `RECORD_AUDIO` | To record your vocal part |
| `MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS` | To configure audio routing (e.g. Bluetooth headset) |
| `BLUETOOTH_CONNECT`, `BLUETOOTH_SCAN` | To detect and use Bluetooth headsets for recording |
| `INTERNET` | To communicate with Google Drive and Firebase |

---

## Data retention and deletion

Your recordings and metadata live in your Google Drive account. You can delete them at any time through the SingStack app or directly in Google Drive.

Your nickname and voice part are stored only on your device in the app's local storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Crash report data is retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days per Google's standard policy.

---

## Children's privacy

SingStack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
